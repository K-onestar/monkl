Monki is the fun side of fashion. Monki online shop gives you dresses, jeans, wide leg trousers, oversized t-shirts, vegan leather platform sandals, chunky heels, accessories, and sooo much more – all with that special Monki-twist to them, and all designed to make you feel awesome. <3
Monki is also a purpose-driven fashion brand that believes in sisterhood, the power of community, and in contributing to a more sustainable fashion industry.
Monki.com delivers to 29 markets, and via International shipping to an additional 64 destinations worldwide. Over 60 concept stores offer the Monki IRL experience across 17 markets. Founded in 2006 in Sweden, Monki is a brand within the H&M group. © Monki
