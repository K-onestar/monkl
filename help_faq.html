ONLINE SHOPPING

How do I track and check the status of my order?

We don’t want to keep you guessing!
You can view the status of your order and track it under ‘My orders’ in the ‘My Account’ section in your Monki profile.

Help! I am locked out of my account.

If you’re having trouble logging into your account, don’t worry!
You can re-set your password by clicking the ‘Forgot password?’ link when you are signing in.
If this doesn’t work, please Contact us. We are happy to help!

When I add an item to my shopping bag, does it guarantee I have the item?

Placing an item into your shopping bag doesn't reserve the item.
In fact, the item could sell out if other customers place orders of the same product before you check out.
If this happens, we will notify you by email that we are unable to ship the item that you have ordered.
And of course, you won't be charged for it.

How long will my order take?

Your order will normally leave our warehouse on the working day after you place your order - or earlier.
e will deliver your purchase within 3-5 working days after receiving your order confirmation.
Federate States of Micronesia, Northen Mariana Islands, Tuvalu, Wallis and Futuna and certain remote places can take 5-6 days.
During high season and Sale, delivery might take a little longer, so please remember to order as early as you can.

Is all the merchandise that is available at Monki stores also available online?

Yes! And even more actually - with our Online exclusives !

I cannot find the item I am looking for, what’s wrong?

At monki.com we keep similar stock as our stores.
If you are unable to find an item at this time, please check back soon as we continuously strive to re-stock.

Where do I add my discount code?

The discount code is to be added in your shopping bag before proceeding to checkout.
Click on “Got a discount code?” located just above your order value.
Then type in the code and click ”Add” to update the total amount of your order.
Remember that you can’t combine the code with other discounts.
Also, please note that the code is case sensitive.
For example, if your discount code is ”DISCOUNT”, the code will not work if you type in ”discount” instead.
Want to remove the code for some reason? Simply press “Remove”.

RETURN AND REFUND

Can I return items I bought?

Yes, you can return items for a 25 USD fee that will be deducted from your refund when the return is processed.
If you aren't satisfied with your order, you can return it within 30 days - and get a full refund within two weeks.
We ask that items are returned clean, unworn and have all their tags on.
For hygiene reasons, underwear briefs, bodies and pierced jewellery cannot be returned or exchanged unless faulty, and all swimwear must still have the hygiene strip.
Items bought online cannot be refunded or exchanged in any of our stores.
Read all details on our Return & refund page.

When will I receive my refund?

We always try to be as fast as possible, but during busy periods (such as gift giving holidays and sales) it can take a little longer. Paypal You can expect to receive a refund within 14 days of sending it back to us.
Once we have processed the return you will receive a return confirmation email. Klarna You will receive confirmation of your return booking within 14 days of returning it back to us.
If you have not received your return confirmation from Monki within that time, please contact Monki customer services.
For any questions relating to your refund, please contact Klarna directly.
All refund information will come directly to you from Klarna.
Paid with 'Pay Now' -You can expect to receive a refund confirmation email from Klarna soon after your return is processed, your refund will be visible in your bank account within 14 days but is usually must faster than this.

Is there anything I can't return?

For hygiene reasons, underwear briefs, bodies and pierced jewellery cannot be returned or exchanged unless faulty, and all swimwear must still have the hygiene strip.

Can I return to store?

Items bought online cannot be refunded or exchanged in any of our stores.

Do you offer exchanges?

Not at the moment. If you need a different size or colour, please return the items and place a new order.

SIZES AND GARMENTS

Do you have any size charts on the website?

Indeed we do!
Check out our Size guide.
We’ve also put together a Monki fit guide with descriptions and short videos to show you exactly how our three different fits looks when you wear them. Learn more here.

When you use the word "Oversized" in a product description, what does that mean?

We know how important it is for a top, jumper or skirt to fit as it should, so we do try to take time to let you know about its measurements.
When you see the word oversized in our text we are saying the coat, shirt, coat, blouse or other Monki pieces are not fitted and are tailored to look slightly larger.
We love the oversized look and believe it is part of the Monki silhouette.
If you are looking for the garment to hug your body a little bit more grab the size down.
Want to learn more? Check out our Size guide, or visit our Fit guide to see a short video of how the oversized fit looks when you wear it.

Where can I find out more about a garment on your site?

Added bits of information about a garment or accessory can be found in the highlighted "Description" section next to each item.

Will I find fur products in your range?

We don’t sell any fur products at all, except faux fur (which is fake).
We also don’t sell merino wool, down or feathers - unless we’re certain they’re cruelty free.
Also, we don’t sell angora at all any more.

What about leather?

The absolute majority of our leather products are faux leather (also called fake leather or fleather).
We do have a strict policy for the real leather products that you will find in our assortment, and that is to only use material that is a by-product of the meat industry.
You can always check the “Details” section of each item to see if a product is a faux leather or a real leather product.
